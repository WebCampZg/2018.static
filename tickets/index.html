SOLD-OUT

This year's demand for tickets exceeded all our expectations. We've filled our venue capacity even before Regular ticket deadline. If you were unable to get your ticket in time, we're sorry, and invite you to grab your ticket earlier next year (or better yet, get an Early Bird!).

We're humbled, honored, and excited by your interest. See you on WebCamp Zagreb!

Conference tickets are sold through Entrio.

All tickets include entrance to both days of the conference, including lunch, drinks, coffee and snacks and drinkup with plenty of craft beer after the conference.

Early bird and Regular tickets include a swag bag with a WebCamp T-Shirt and various other goodies and sponsored gifts. Late bird tickets do not, so don't delay until the last moment.

Community vote

As was the case for the last three years, ticket holders will get a chance to vote on a number of submitted talks and decide which talks end up in the official talks roster. If you own a ticket, you will receive a notification when the voting opens, after the first round of spakers have been chosen.

Payment methods

Entrio accepts payment by credit card or bank transfer. Please note that bank transfers will be disabled a week before the conference, to make sure all charges are processed before the conference starts.

Don't feel like paying?

All speakers who speak at one of our meetups in the period since WebCamp Zagreb 2017 until this year's conference will get a free ticket.

To save your money, submit a talk to one of our user groups today: Frontman Zagreb, Javascript Zagreb, Lambda Zagreb, Python Hrvatska, RubyZG, Testival, Web Designers Hrvatska, Zagreb Software Testing Club, or ZgPHP.
