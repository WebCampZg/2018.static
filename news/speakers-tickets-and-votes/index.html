11.07.2018.

Of speakers, tickets, and votes

In which speakers are announced, community vote starts and early bird tickets are nigh.

Speakers announced

After careful deliberation, the talk committee examined all 206 submissions and selected 24 talks to appear on stage at WebCamp Zagreb 2018. The competition was tough, so congratulations to our speakers.

They will be joined by 2 keynotes, 3 sponsored talks, and 3 talks chosen by you, our ticket holders in the community vote. More on that below.

View the talks!

End of early bird tickets

The last day to get tickets at the amazing price of 300 kn is this Sunday, 2018-07-15. Regular tickets cost twice as much, so don’t miss out.

As an added benefit early bird ticket holders get to choose the final three talks in the community vote.

Get your ticket!

Community vote is open

As has become customary, all early bird ticket holders get a chance to influence the conference lineup by voting for talk submissions which were highly rated, but didn’t quite make the cut.

If you have an early bird ticket, you will receive an email invite to the community vote soon. You may vote for as many or as few talks as you wish.

The vote closes on Sunday, 2018-07-22 and three top rated talks will join our speaker lineup.

Check out the vote!
