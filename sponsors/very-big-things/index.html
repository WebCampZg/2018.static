Backend developer + devops

Location: Zagreb, Croatia

Very Big Things designs & develops digital experiences for web, mobile, VR, AR and any other device you come across.

Most of our projects wouldn't be possible without a reliable, consistent and extendable backend service - which we achieve by using Elixir language along with the Phoenix framework.

Most of our apps are powered by GraphQL APIs and other battle-tested technologies like Postgres and Redis. We deploy apps to Heroku and manage AWS with Terraform.
